WebGL Material & Shading Demonstration

Teaching Project: Interactive demonstration of different shading models (Phong vs Blinn-Phong) and how materials interact with light to create different visual appearances.

Learning Objectives:
• Understand how Phong shading (spec: H·N) vs Blinn-Phong shading (spec: N·H) differ
• Observe how material properties (ambient, diffuse, specular, shininess) affect appearance
• See how light position changes lighting conditions
• Compare different geometric primitives (Torus, Vase, Sphere)
